Volo

Volo is an icon theme for retina iOS devices, and is availble on the Cydia store. Volo includes over 300 hand crafted icons, in both solid and transparent styles. UI elements across the system have also been themed, such as within Control Center and the Settings app. Volo has recieved over 15,000 cumulative downloads. (some more legal than others ðŸ˜…)

Since the begining, Volo has been very important to me. It was the first major product that I had shipped to the public, and I wanted it to be the best that it could. I spent many hours taking user requests for additional icons, and creating multiple styles and variations until I finally decided on the style. All icons were created as vectors, resulting in an easy adaptation to each screen size, and streamlining future updates when new devices are released.
