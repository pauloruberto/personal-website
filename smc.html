SMC Orientation

This website was developed for St. Micheals College at the University of Toronto, as the main landing page for information regarding orientation week 2015. Details on registration, scheduling, and more were all present for students to view. Every incoming student regularily visited this webpage to obtain info over the course of the week.

St. Micheals College reached out to me, asking me to design and develop the website, while working alongside the orientation co-ordinators. As the project developed, and the requirements evolved, I worked closely with the co-ordinators to meet their needs and desires.
